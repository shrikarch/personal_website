Owner: Vishal Kadam

Background:

Built for a professional photographer to showcase his work, this website was another small scale portfolio website.
The objective was to have a front page that displays number of categories to choose from and a contact form to reach out to the photographer/owener of the site.

HTML and CSS3 based markup with SCSS as pre-processor.
PHP (phpmailer) was used for form submission
