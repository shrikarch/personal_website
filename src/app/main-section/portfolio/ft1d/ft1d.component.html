Jason Gensler's 670G Experience | Design and Development

Owner: Jason Gensler

Background:

Jason Gensler is a Type 1 Diabetes patient and aspires to be a guide to the people who are new to the world of Type 1 Diabetes. He is successfully leading a Type 1 community on Facebook whose objective is to bring the people together in order to help each other strive and battle the diabetes easily than ever.
This website was built to showcase his experience with Medtronic's 670G artificial pancreas.

HTML and CSS3 based markup with Less as pre-processor.
PHP ws used to modularise the entire markup.
